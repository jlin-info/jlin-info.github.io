Jun-Wei Lin, PhD in Software Engineering, UC Irvine

I am a software engineer, researcher, best-selling author, and top-rated instructor. Currently, I work at MGM Resorts International and support ths Site Reliability Engineering team. Prior to MGM, I received my PhD in Software Engineering at the School of ICS, University of California, Irvine, advised by Prof. Sam Malek. My research interest is software analysis and testing with natural language processing and machine learning techniques (AI for SE).
