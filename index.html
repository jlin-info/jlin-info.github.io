Jun-Wei Lin, PhD in Software Engineering, UC Irvine

I am a software engineer and researcher at MGM Resorts International. Prior to MGM, I received my PhD in Software Engineering at the School of ICS, University of California, Irvine, advised by Prof. Sam Malek. My research interest is software analysis and testing with natural language processing and machine learning techniques.
